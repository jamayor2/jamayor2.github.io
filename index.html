Research

My research is focused on quantifying the soil moisture and evapotranspiration in dryland ecosystems (Southwestern US) through distributed hydrological simulations, remote sensing, and field measurements within the Jornada Experimental Range of the U.S. Department of Agriculture
in southern New Mexico, U.S.
The model inputs can be summarized into terrain, atmospheric forcing, soil, and vegetation parameters.
